Arabic Grammar

Praise be to Allah, the Lord of the worlds and the prayers and blessings be upon the noblest of the Prophets and Messengers, Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ), his household and all his companions.

Al-Hidaayah Academy was started in the year 2010 in the month of May by a group of dedicated Muslimahs to fulfill the needs of the Muslim Community to impart Islamic knowledge through the teachings of the Holy Qur’an and the Sunnah of our Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) under the careful guidance and supervision of Madani scholars.

The Academy also believes in dispelling misconceptions about Islam amongst the Muslims so as to encourage peace and unity for the benefit of the society and the country.

Our Objectives

  • To promote pure Islamic values and thinking on sound knowledge.
  • Nurture love of our beloved Prophet (ﷺ) and follow his Sunnah in our day to day lives.
  • Understand the Qu’ran directly in Arabic.
  • Perform ‘Ibaadah (worship) in its true spirit with love.
  • Develop the art of constructive pondering and reflection.
  • Acquire the necessary life skills for effective time, anger and stress management.
  • Find the motivation to serve humanity.
  • Special emphasis is laid on Good Character Building.
" The Academy’s vision and mission is to spread Islamic awareness among Muslim women and through them to their families and ultimately to the Ummah. Thereby, uplifting the Ummah and helping in the development of the nation. "

General Curriculum at Al Hidaayah Academy

  • Aqeedah / Tawheed.
  • Complete explanation (Tafseer) of the Holy Qur’an in Urdu and English.
  • Tajweed (Qur’an Recitation).
  • Seerah of the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ).
  • Usool Al-Hadith – Methodology of hadith evaluation.
  • Muslim Heroes.
  • Arabic Grammar.
  • Hifz
  • Duas from the Qur’an and Sunnah
  • Dawah Training Program.
  • Good Character Building.
